# Community Overview

Welcome to the BMad Method community! We're a growing group of developers, designers, and innovators who believe in **Human Amplification, Not Replacement** — bringing out the best thinking in both humans and AI through guided collaboration.

## Our Philosophy

BMad strengthens human-AI collaboration through specialized agents and guided workflows. Every contribution and conversation should answer: **"Does this make humans and AI better together?"**

**What drives us:**
- Enhanced collaboration patterns and workflows
- Improved agent personas and prompts
- Domain-specific modules leveraging BMad Core
- Better planning and context continuity

**What we avoid:**
- Purely automated solutions that sideline humans
- Complexity that creates barriers to adoption
- Features that fragment BMad Core's foundation
